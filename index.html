Strategy Guide

HIT (Take Card)

Take an additional card when your hand value is low or dealer has a strong card (7-A)

STAND (Stay)

Keep your current hand when value is sufficient or dealer shows weak card (2-6)

DOUBLE (Double Down)

Double your bet when you have a strong starting hand (9-11) and dealer shows weakness

SPLIT (Split Pair)

Split matching cards to play two separate hands, especially for Aces and 8s
